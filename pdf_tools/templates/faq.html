Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Kannanware Innovation? +
Kannanware Innovations LLP is an Indian company specializing in SAP® Finance consulting, corporate training, and managed staffing solutions. Established in 2021 and based in Chennai, the company helps businesses with finance process consulting, re-engineering, and digital transformation. It also offers training programs to bridge skill gaps in the tech industry. With a focus on agility, transparency, and security, Kannanware aims to deliver innovative SAP® solutions that drive business growth and efficiency.
Is this tool free to use? +
Yes, our basic PDF processing tools are free to use. However, some advanced features may require a premium subscription.
How can I merge multiple PDFs? +
Go to the Merge PDF page, upload multiple PDFs, and click the "Merge PDFs" button. The system will combine them into a single file.
How do I extract text from scanned PDFs? +
Use our OCR (Extract Text from Image PDF) tool. Upload your scanned PDF, and our system will extract the text using Optical Character Recognition.
How secure is my data? +
Security is our top priority. Your files are processed locally and not stored or shared with anyone.
Can I use this tool on my mobile phone? +
Yes! Our platform is mobile-friendly, and you can access all features on your smartphone or tablet.
